Dictation 9642






With regard to Exempt Resolution No. 468 of September 23, 1992, which approved the budget for 1992 of the Welfare Service of that Institution, this Superintendence informs you that it did not authorize the amount of $ 3,900,000, requested In Title XII "Transfers Expenses", item F "Optional Benefits", allocation f) "Easter Celebration", because the allocation of resources to this type of activities is not contemplated in the Regulation of that Welfare Service
